About Tunde Obi

Minister, author, and professional committed to inspiring spiritual growth and excellence in every sphere of life

My Story

I am a devoted minister of the gospel, known for my unwavering passion for living and teaching the Word of God. My pastoral journey began in January 2006 when I led a campus fellowship during my university years, and by April 2012, I was ordained into the pastoral ministry. I currently serve at Enthronement House Christian Centre.

In August 2016, I graduated with distinction from The Redeemed Christian Bible College, earning an Advanced Diploma in Christian Leadership—a testament to my dedication to ministry and spiritual growth. Beyond the pulpit, I am a seasoned professional in the corporate world with over 14 years of experience in internal audit across finance, insurance, telecoms, and other sectors. My expertise has been pivotal in uncovering fraud, recovering losses, and strengthening governance in multinational corporations.

I'm married to Oluwabukunola and blessed with our daughter, Joy Araoluwa. As a devoted husband, father, and servant of God, I'm passionate about advancing the agendas of the Kingdom of God, striving to collaborate with like-minded and spirit-filled believers to fulfill God's purposes, bringing the same dedication and integrity to every sphere of my life—family, ministry, and career.

Professional Background

With over 14 years in the corporate world, I've developed extensive expertise in internal audit across multiple sectors including finance, insurance, and telecommunications.

My professional work focuses on:

  • Uncovering fraud and implementing preventative measures
  • Recovering financial losses for organizations
  • Strengthening governance structures in multinational corporations
  • Developing and implementing audit frameworks and controls
  • Mentoring professionals in ethical business practices

I believe in bringing the same commitment to excellence that defines my spiritual endeavors into my professional work, creating an integrated approach to leadership and service.
