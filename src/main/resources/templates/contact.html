Frequently Asked Questions

What topics do you typically speak on?

My speaking topics include biblical meditation, spiritual growth, faith for young believers, integrity in the workplace, purpose-driven living, and leadership from a biblical perspective. I can also develop custom messages tailored to your specific event theme.

Do you offer mentoring or counseling services?

Yes, I provide mentoring to young believers and professionals seeking to integrate their faith with their career and life purpose. Due to time constraints, I offer structured mentoring programs rather than one-off sessions. Please contact me for more details.

How can I order your books in bulk for my organization?

Bulk orders are available at discounted rates for churches, schools, and organizations. Please contact orders@tundeobi.com with details about which book(s) you're interested in and the quantity needed. We can also arrange for signed copies for special events.

How far in advance should I book you for speaking engagements?

For optimal scheduling, I recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for weekend events. However, I understand that needs sometimes arise unexpectedly, so please don't hesitate to reach out even for shorter notice requests. I'll do my best to accommodate your event if my schedule permits.
